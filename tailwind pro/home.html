Entering the World of Strays


India is a home to around 9.1 million stray cats living in streets

And around 2.7 million cats die each year due to many reasons

These reasons may include disease, starvation, injuries, accidents, ets.

This is the time for us as the citizens of India to take good care of stray animals


And provide them basic necessities like food, water and shelter from rain.
